Controls:
  W - Move forward
  A - Move left
  S - Move backward
  D - Move right
  Space - Move Up
  Z - Move Down
  Shift (hold) - Sprint
  G - Press to spawn a single frame sphere at spawn (Holding down the key
      continues to spawn single frame spheres)

  Use mouse to look around scene. Objects in green have no depth testing,
  objects in blue have it, and objects in gold have "xray" depth testing (both).
